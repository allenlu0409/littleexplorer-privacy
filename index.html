小小探索家 隱私政策
LittleExplorer Privacy Policy

生效日期 / Effective date: 2026-07-11

中文

「小小探索家」是為幼兒設計的認知學習應用程式。我們極度重視兒童隱私：

  1. 不收集任何個人資料。本 App 不要求註冊，不收集姓名、電子郵件、位置、裝置識別碼或任何其他個人資訊。
  2. 所有資料僅存於裝置。學習紀錄（瀏覽次數、遊戲成績、貼紙）與家長設定僅儲存在您裝置的本地儲存空間，絕不上傳至任何伺服器。
  3. 無廣告、無追蹤、無分析工具。本 App 不包含任何廣告、第三方追蹤或分析 SDK。
  4. 無外部連結與內購。App 內沒有通往外部網站的連結，也沒有任何應用程式內購買。
  5. 家長門檻。家長專區（統計、時間限制、設定）以數學題門檻保護，避免幼兒誤入。
  6. 資料刪除。解除安裝 App 即完全刪除所有本地資料。

如有任何疑問，請聯絡：softwareunify0409@gmail.com

English

LittleExplorer is a cognitive learning app designed for toddlers. We take children's privacy extremely seriously:

  1. No personal data is collected. The app requires no registration and collects no names, emails, location, device identifiers, or any other personal information.
  2. All data stays on the device. Learning records (view counts, quiz scores, stickers) and parental settings are stored only in your device's local storage and are never uploaded to any server.
  3. No ads, no tracking, no analytics. The app contains no advertising, third-party tracking, or analytics SDKs.
  4. No external links or in-app purchases.
  5. Parental gate. The parents' area (statistics, time limits, settings) is protected by an arithmetic gate to prevent access by young children.
  6. Data deletion. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data.
